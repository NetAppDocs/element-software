= Create a QoS policy
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
You can create QoS policies and apply them when creating volumes.

. Select *Management* > *QoS Policies*.
. Click *Create QoS Policy*.
. Enter the *Policy Name*.
. Enter the**Min IOPS**, *Max IOPS*, and *Burst IOPS* values.
. Click *Create QoS Policy*.
